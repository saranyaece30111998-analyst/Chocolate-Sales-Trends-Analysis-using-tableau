# 🍫 Chocolate Sales Analysis – Tableau Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview
This project analyzes chocolate sales data across multiple countries, products, and salespersons.  
The interactive Tableau dashboard consolidates shipment volumes, revenue, product performance, and salesperson contributions into a single view.

**Objectives:**
- Evaluate overall chocolate sales performance across regions  
- Identify top-performing products and categories  
- Assess salesperson contributions and highlight best performers  
- Uncover shipment and revenue trends  
- Provide actionable insights for market expansion and product strategy  

---

## 🛠 Tools Used
- **Tableau** → Dashboard creation & visualization  
- **Excel** → Data cleaning & preprocessing  

---

## 📊 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
- **Total Boxes Shipped:** 1,77,007  
- **Total Sales Amount:** ₹61,83,625  
- **Top Country by Sales:** Australia  
- **Top Product Category:** Dark Chocolate Variants (50%, 70%, 85%, 99%)  
- **Top Salesperson:** Ches Bonnell (~₹300K sales)

## 🔍 SMART Analytics Approach

### 1. **Descriptive Analysis**
- Total shipments: 1,77,007 boxes  
- Revenue: ₹61.8M  
- Australia leads in sales; Dark chocolate dominates product mix  
- Ches Bonnell is the top-performing salesperson  

### 2. **Diagnostic Analysis**
- Premium dark chocolates drive demand  
- Australia’s dominance reflects strong distribution & consumer preference  
- Moderate sales in India & UK due to limited promotions  
- USA, Canada, NZ show weaker brand penetration  

### 3. **Predictive Analysis**
- Dark chocolate demand expected to rise (health-conscious trend)  
- India & UK show growth potential  
- Specialty products (e.g., Manuka Honey Choco) may gain traction  
- Seasonal peaks (festivals, holidays) will boost sales  

### 4. **Prescriptive Analysis**
- Expand premium dark chocolate lines  
- Strengthen marketing in India & UK  
- Introduce promotions in USA, Canada, NZ  
- Incentivize top performers & train weaker salespersons  
- Diversify portfolio with innovative flavors  

---

## ✅ Conclusion
The analysis reveals strong overall performance with **₹61.8M revenue** and **1.77 lakh boxes shipped**, driven by Australia’s market dominance and premium dark chocolate demand.  
Salesperson contributions highlight clear leaders like **Ches Bonnell**, while underperforming regions (USA, Canada, NZ) present untapped opportunities for growth.
